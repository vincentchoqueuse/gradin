Privacy First: Gradin is built with privacy at its core. All your data stays on your device and your personal iCloud (if enabled). We never access, collect, or share your information with third parties.

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Gradin ("we," "our," or "the app") handles your information. Gradin is designed to give you complete control over your data with no server-side collection or processing.

Data We Do NOT Collect

Gradin does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Student names, emails, or identification numbers
  • Student photos or personal information
  • Assessment data, grades, or comments
  • Course information or learning outcomes
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any personal information whatsoever

How Your Data Is Stored

Local Storage

All data you enter into Gradin is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Student Information: Names, emails, school IDs, groups, and optional photos
  • Course Data: Course names, levels, dates, and associated information
  • Learning Outcomes: Outcome definitions, criteria, and validation thresholds
  • Assessments: Grades, comments, and assessment dates

iCloud Storage (Optional)

Gradin offers optional iCloud synchronization through Apple's CloudKit service. When enabled:

  • Your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Data syncs automatically across your devices signed in with the same Apple ID
  • We have no access to your iCloud data
  • iCloud sync can be disabled at any time in the app's Settings
  • Apple's iCloud terms and privacy policy apply to cloud-stored data

Photo Library Access

Gradin requests access to your photo library solely to allow you to select student photos for identification purposes. When you grant photo access:

  • You can select individual photos to associate with student profiles
  • Photos are stored locally on your device using external storage for efficiency
  • Photos are never uploaded to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • If iCloud sync is enabled, photos sync to your personal iCloud account
  • You can deny photo access and the app will function normally without student photos

Data Exports

Gradin allows you to export your data in CSV format. When you export:

  • Data is saved as a file on your device
  • You control where the file is saved and who has access to it
  • No data is transmitted to us or any third party during export

Data Imports

You can import student data from CSV files. When you import:

  • Data is read from the file you select and stored locally in the app
  • The original file is not modified
  • No data is transmitted to us or any third party during import

Your Rights and Control

Data Ownership

You own all data you enter into Gradin. We never claim ownership or access to your information.

Data Access

You have complete access to all your data at all times through the app interface and can export it to CSV format.

Data Deletion

You can delete any data within the app:

  • Delete individual assessments, students, outcomes, or courses
  • Uninstall the app to permanently delete all local data
  • Disable iCloud sync and manually delete data from iCloud settings

Data Portability

Export your data to CSV format at any time for use in other systems or backup purposes.

Educational Privacy Compliance

FERPA Compliance (United States)

Gradin complies with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) because:

  • All data is stored locally under the teacher's control
  • We do not access, process, or store student educational records
  • Teachers maintain full control over data access and sharing

GDPR Compliance (European Union)

Gradin complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) because:

  • We are not a data controller or processor (no data collection)
  • Users have complete control over their data (right to access)
  • Users can delete all data at any time (right to erasure)
  • Users can export data in a standard format (right to data portability)
  • No profiling or automated decision-making occurs

Children's Privacy

Gradin is designed for use by teachers and educators, not by children. While the app may store information about students (including minors), it does not collect this data from children directly. Teachers are responsible for ensuring they have appropriate authorization to maintain student records.

Third-Party Services

Gradin does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The only third-party service is Apple's CloudKit (if iCloud sync is enabled), which is covered by Apple's privacy policy.

Security

Data security is provided by:

  • iOS Encryption: All local data is protected by iOS device encryption
  • iCloud Encryption: Cloud data is encrypted by Apple's CloudKit service
  • No Network Transmission: No data is sent to external servers
  • Device Protection: Standard iOS security features (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode) protect access to the app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
